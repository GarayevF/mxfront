COMMON ISSUES

Common Refrigerator
Problems We Fix

From cooling failures to strange noises, we diagnose and repair the most common refrigerator issues quickly.

Not Cooling Properly

Your fridge isn't maintaining the right temperature, causing food to spoil faster than it should.

Ice Buildup / Frost

Excessive frost or ice accumulation in the freezer compartment affecting performance and storage space.

Water Leaking

Water pooling under or inside your refrigerator from clogged drains, damaged water lines, or faulty seals.

Strange Noises

Unusual buzzing, clicking, or humming sounds coming from your refrigerator indicating mechanical issues.

Door Seal Issues

Worn or damaged door gaskets allowing warm air in, causing the compressor to overwork and waste energy.

Temperature Fluctuations

Inconsistent temperatures that cause freezing in the fridge or thawing in the freezer, risking food safety.
